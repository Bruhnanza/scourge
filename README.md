If you've ever played RLCraft or a heavily modded pack from the early days of Minecraft modding, you know the feeling—a world that felt genuinely dangerous, where progression was slow and every small win mattered. Scourge was built to bring that back. Running on Fabric for a leaner, more modern experience, it strips away the comfort of vanilla progression and rebuilds it from the ground up. Familiar milestones take longer to reach, with new steps and challenges woven between each one. You'll need to venture into overhauled terrain and entirely new biomes, uncover hidden structures scattered across a hostile world, and face new creatures and bosses that demand real preparation. It's not about punishing you for the sake of it — it's about making every breakthrough feel like it means something, and bringing back the weight of a world that feels alive and worth exploring.

### Features
- Overhauled World Generation (Biomes O' Plenty, Biomes We've Gone, Regions Unexplored)
- Challenge Vast Dungeons (When Dungeons Arise, Epic Structures, Bosses of Mass Destruction)
- New & Renewed Structures (Battle Towers, Luki's Mods, Moog's Structures)
- Redesigned Dimensions (Better Nether, Better End, Deeper and Darker)
- Expanded Equipment & Tools (Artifacts, Bronze, Mythic Metals)
- Lots of QoL Improvements (Antique Atlas, Traveler's Backpack, Waystones)
- RPG Elements (Better Combat, LevelZ, Tough as Nails)
- Custom Datapack with Features & Tweaks (Recipes, Enchantments, & more!)
- Performance Improvements & OptiFine-like Enhancements (Based on Fabulously Optimized)
